About Us

We are Class 27, the students of Victoria College set to graduate in 2027.
As one of the most distinguished classes in the history
of Victoria College, the prestigious College of Princes,
we take pride in the excellence that defines us. Our class
is united in our commitment to personal growth, mutual support, and effective
learning. We aim to build a strong community where each of us thrives, all while making
unforgettable memories along the way. With determination and
collaboration, we strive to make our time at Victoria College truly remarkable.

Welcome to our digital space where learning, fun, and memories come
together! This website keeps us connected, informed, and engaged throughout
the school year. Whether you're looking for class updates, getting curricular support,
or celebrating birthdays, you’ll find it all here!.

What do we offer?

Announcements

The Announcements section keeps all students informed about class activities, upcoming events, and important reminders. This is where students can read about deadlines, changes in the schedule, and other class-wide information. It’s the go-to place to stay updated on what’s happening in the class community.

Birthday boy

Birthday boy is a fun and engaging way to celebrate your students' birthdays. It features a personalized birthday card, birthday party invitations, and a virtual birthday party. The birthday boy platform allows students to easily share their birthday information, create birthday cards, and invite friends to their birthday party.
