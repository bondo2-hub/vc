About Us

We are Class 27, the students of Victoria College set to graduate in 2027.
As one of the most distinguished classes in the history
of Victoria College, the prestigious College of Princes,
we take pride in the excellence that defines us. Our class
is united in our commitment to personal growth, mutual support, and effective
learning. We aim to build a strong community where each of us thrives, all while making
unforgettable memories along the way. With determination and
collaboration, we strive to make our time at Victoria College truly remarkable.

Welcome to our digital space where learning, fun, and memories come
together! This website keeps us connected, informed, and engaged throughout
the school year. Whether you're looking for class updates, getting curricular support,
or celebrating birthdays, you’ll find it all here!.

What do we offer?

Announcements

The Announcements section is your go-to place for staying informed about our class updates, events, and important news. From event reminders to changes in schedules, this section ensures every student is aware of the latest happenings in the class. Think of it as a dynamic bulletin board for the whole class.

Birthday boy

The Birthday Boy section is all about celebrating your classmates on their special day. It highlights whose birthday is coming up and allows classmates to leave fun messages or wishes. It’s a fun way to add some joy and make every birthday feel extra special.

Studying

The Studying section is a dedicated space for all academic resources. Students can find assignments, project details, study guides, and exam schedules, helping everyone stay organized and on top of their workload. This section ensures that all essential study materials are accessible in one convenient place, promoting better academic success.

Shoutouts

The Shoutouts section is where classmates can celebrate each other’s achievements and milestones. Whether it’s excelling in academics, winning a competition, or making a meaningful contribution to the class, this section is all about giving recognition. It also features honorable mentions for students who make an impact, inspiring positivity and motivation among peers.

Shop

The Shop is where students can buy class-related merchandise, such as T-shirts, hoodies, mugs, or notebooks. This section is perfect for showing off class spirit and supporting class activities through fundraising. The shop features high-quality designs that students will love to own.

Class 27

Class 27 is a feature designed to help classmates get to know each other better. It includes a profile for every student in the class, highlighting their unique characteristics, achievements, and fun facts. This section fosters a sense of connection and belonging, allowing students to learn more about their peers in a meaningful way.
